## Summary

The Palo Alto City Council held a special meeting on February 1, 2026, primarily to conduct a lengthy Closed Session. During this session, council members discussed matters related to the City Attorney position, including a potential appointment and labor negotiations. After nearly six hours of private discussion, the Council returned to open session, and Mayor Veenker announced that no reportable action was taken. The meeting then adjourned shortly thereafter.

*   The City Council convened for a special meeting.
*   The majority of the meeting time was spent in a Closed Session.
*   Discussions in Closed Session focused on the appointment of a City Attorney and labor negotiations concerning the City Attorney.
*   No public decisions or actions were reported following the Closed Session.

## Agenda Highlights

**1. Public Employee Appointment (City Attorney)**
The Council met in a private session to discuss the potential appointment of an individual to the City Attorney position. These types of discussions are held confidentially to protect the privacy of individuals involved in personnel matters. No public decision was announced regarding this item.

**2. Conference with Labor Negotiators (City Attorney)**
Council members also met privately with labor negotiators to discuss terms related to the City Attorney position. This item typically involves confidential discussions about employment conditions or contracts. Following this discussion, no public action or agreement was reported.
