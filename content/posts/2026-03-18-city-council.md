## Summary

Council convened at 6:00 p.m. and took the following actions:
- Approved the consent calendar as recommended.
- Held a study session on the draft Climate Action Plan and directed staff to incorporate feedback.
- Adopted Resolution 2026-XX authorizing the City Manager to execute the contract for the California Avenue streetscape project.
- Continued the discussion on the proposed ADU ordinance to the next meeting.
Public comment was received on several items.
